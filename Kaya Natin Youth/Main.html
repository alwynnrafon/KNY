About Us

Kaya Natin Youth is a movement of young Filipinos who believe in the power of good governance, ethical leadership, and active citizenship. We are a community built on the idea that change starts with the youth, and that every Filipino has the ability to make a positive impact in society when guided by integrity, compassion, and service.

Our mission is to inspire and empower the next generation of leaders by providing opportunities for education, advocacy, and community engagement. Through workshops, outreach programs, and collaborative projects, we aim to develop the values of responsibility, inclusiveness, and accountability among the youth.

As part of the larger Kaya Natin! movement for good governance, Kaya Natin Youth continues to champion initiatives that strengthen communities, amplify youth voices, and encourage active participation in nation-building. We believe that real change is possible when young people are equipped with the right knowledge, skills, and support system to become servant leaders.
